Why do we gather in places like this?

Thousands of people,
at the same time,
for the same moment.

A stadium turns strangers
into a single reaction.

One tension.
One release.
One shared pulse.

For a few hours,
it doesn’t matter who you are.

You become part of something bigger.

Something louder.
Something emotional.
Something you don’t fully control.

So what are we really looking for there?

The game
or the feeling of belonging to something greater than ourselves?
